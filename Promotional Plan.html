- Promotional Plan -

Our promotional plan involves two independent promotional methods aside from using the website that will be disseminated in and out of the school, which will be vital for gathering the viewers that we need for our podcast live streaming.

Promotional Emails

To maximize and use our APEC Schools accounts, we will be using collective emails to reach our target audiences. Teenagers being our main target for an audience works well with this method since everyone studying here should be teenagers at the very least. The downside to this method is that emails like this often get covered up by other emails, and that is why multiple daily uploads might be necessary to balance the issue at hand. Another disadvantage is that it is limited to APEC students.

Procedure:

  1. 1. Prepare the promotional materials required for this method.
  2. 2. Insert the promotional materials in an email.
  3. 3. Use an eye-catching subject for the said email for more engagement.
  4. 4. Gather the emails for all the sections that fit the target audience.
  5. 5. Create a schedule for deciding the times the invitation will be sent.
  6. 6. Follow that schedule and make sure that it is reaching the audience

Social Media Promotion

The majority of the world’s population engages in the use of social media, with teenagers being the most active users. To use this to our advantage, we will be using social media platforms where we can utilize our podcast accounts as well as our accounts.The only disadvantage that we need to work on for this method would be finding a way to reach other people easier. The social media world is very wide and it is important to post effectively to reach the right people.

Procedure:

  1. 1. Prepare the promotional materials required for this method.
  2. 2. Open the podcast's social media accounts.
  3. 3. Create an engaging caption or use the tagline as one.
  4. 4. Post the promotional materials after checking for mistakes.
  5. 5. Open our accounts for personal purposes.
  6. 6. Share the post from the main podcast’s social media account.
